VISION & MISION


Vision

We are a contractor company that always:

1. Always trying to improve science and technology / skills / creativity

2. Committed to providing satisfaction and trust to clients with the best quality

3. Finished the project with Zero Accident


Mision

1. Pursuit of Client Satisfaction

We continue to innovate our technologies and cultivate creativity to provide high quality construction works and services in response to the needs and trust of clients and the society.

2. Enhancement of Shareholder Value

We make sustainable business development by thoroughly efficient management and maintaining profitability to boost the shareholder value along with the corporate value.

3. Respect for Employees' Vitality

We create an open-minded and rewarding company where the employees can fully exercise their ability and individuality.

4. Social Emphasis

We practice fair corporate activities and aim to become a good corporate citizen which the society can trust.

5. Contribution to Global Environment

We constantly seek to be an eco and human friendly contractor and also value harmony between living environment and nature.
